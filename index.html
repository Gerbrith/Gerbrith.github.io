About

I am a lot of things. I am a nerd, a mother, a viking enthusiast, a roleplayer, an amateur origamist, an entirely normal weirdo. With a BSc in Business administration and a Msc in teaching business at a High school level, I worked in tech service until I got my dream job; teaching kids how to make stuff in a Fablab. Since the fall of 2017 I've been teaching at Framhaldsskólin í Vestmannaeyjum. Now it's time to expand my skillset, improve my understanding of the possibilities of Fablab and make myself a better teacher in the process.
