About

Here's my picture.

Originally from the Faroe Islands, I now live in the Westman Islands. In between, I attended an international school in Norway and lived in Reykjavik for ten years. I graduated with a BSc in Business administration because I like organizing things, and because I disagree with the way our societies work right now. I think we, as a species, need to reconsider our priorities. I chose to get a MSc in teaching Business at a high school level, hoping to teach young people how to start and run their own businesses, because I believe that this will give them a better future than relying on major corporations to provide job security.
